Broadband Under 10 Mbps + Low Opportunity

All census tracts include at least one census block with broadband speeds below 10 Mbps and are located in areas with low opportunity indicators.

Broadband 10 Mbps to 25 Mbps + Low Opportunity

All census tracts include at least one census block with broadband speeds between 10 Mbps and 25 Mbps and are located in areas with low opportunity indicators.

Broadband Low-Fiber Deployment + Low Opportunity

Low Fiber census tracts are defined by tracts that have less than 30% of households in the tract with access to fiber broadband and are located in areas with low opportunity indicators. The 30% threshold is based on the the natural breaks using Jenks classification method.

Broadband Fiber Spatial Clustering + Low Opportunity

A spatial analysis examining the percent of households with fiber broadband access using Anselin Local Moran's I to determine tracts that are considered "Low / Low" signifying low broadband access tracts next to other low fiber broadband access tracts
